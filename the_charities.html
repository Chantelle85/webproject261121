Guide Dogs

Guide Dogs is the world's largest breeder and trainer of working dogs. And thanks to their dedicated staff and volunteers and vital donations, 36,000 lives
have been transformed through a guide dog partnership since 1931. Through the sponsor a puppy programme you can be part of their very special journey to
transform the life of someone with sight loss.

The Society For Abandoned Animals

The Society for Abandoned Animals who since 1966 have cared for and have re-homed unwanted cats and rabbits and would never allow a healthy animal to be put down.
They have successfully rehomed 1,399 in the last five years alone. If you wish to support this charity you can do so by volunteering, fundraising, food or financial donations,
sponsoring a rabbit or cat pen and fostering.

Tiny Paws MCR

Tiny Paws MCR is run by a small team of volunteers who take in rodents and rabbits in need and find them loving forever homes with carefully matched adopters.
Reliant on donations from kind members of the public to cover the costs of feeding, housing and veterinary treatment for the small animals in their care.
You can support them by provisions of food, bedding, litter, treats, and cleaning things through their amazon wishlists. Giving direct donations or free donations when you shop online.
